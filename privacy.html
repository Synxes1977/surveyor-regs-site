Privacy Policy

This policy explains how we handle your personal data in accordance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our eligibility checker or contact form, we collect:

  • Name and contact information (Email address).
  • Property details related to unauthorised works.
  • The urgency of your regularisation requirement.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process this data under the lawful basis of Legitimate Interests. Specifically, we use it to provide you with a preliminary assessment of your building regulations compliance and to respond to your inquiries.

3. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or professional RICS reporting requirements.

4. Your Rights

Under UK law, you have the right to request access to your data, request correction or erasure, and the right to object to processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us via the email provided below.

5. Contact Details

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: info@regularisation.co.uk
